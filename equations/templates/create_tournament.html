A tournament is a collection of games which are organized under a group. The creator will be able to assign players to tables, and the players should be able to view their assigned games and go to them with the click of a button. Judges should have a view of all the games going on, and be able to go to the games where players are requesting help. Scores at the end of the round are collected automatically. Tournaments are created by the group's owners. Only members of a group can participate in a group's tournament.
